How to play

  • Select a team in the Teams section.
  • Select a piece (or Single cell) in the Pieces menu.
  • Click on the grid inside your quadrant to place cells or pieces.
  • Press Play to start the simulation.
  • Use Random to generate a new battlefield.
  • Use Step to advance one generation at a time.

Birth, survival and death follow Conway's original rules. The team of each newborn cell is decided by the majority among its alive neighbours.
